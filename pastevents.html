TechLaunchPad'23

The TechLaunchpad - Igniting Tech Careers for First-Year Fellas!

The TechLaunchpad, specially crafted to empower our talented first-year students in the world of technology as it gave Roadmap to newly entered Freshmans.

Amidst the excitement and chaos of the college beginnings, we understood the questions lingering in your minds about “which tech career path to follow”. That's why we hosted The TechLaunchpad, an event that served as a compass to guide you through the diverse tech fields, including IT, Data Science, Development, and Startups.

Our expert speakers, seasoned professionals, and industry insiders came together to share invaluable insights, trends, and cutting-edge technologies that shape the tech landscape.
